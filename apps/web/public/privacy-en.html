Privacy Policy

Raiymbek Park app · Updated 16 July 2026

The Raiymbek Park app is a private space for the residents and apartment owners of the Raiymbek Park residential complex. It is intended for residents of the complex only. This document explains what personal data we collect, why, who can see it, and how to have it deleted.

What we collect

Data you enter when registering and in your profile settings:

Data Why
Name So other residents and the management company know who posted an offer or a request
Phone number Required if you sign in by phone — the confirmation code is sent to it. If you sign in with Google or Facebook it is optional and you may leave the field empty. When a number is given, the management company uses it for emergency contact
Block and apartment number So the management company knows which unit a request refers to
Role (owner or tenant) Determines access to parts of the app
Profile photo Optional. Shown next to your posts and comments
Car plate numbers Optional. So you can be reached if your car is blocking access

Data created as you use the app:

  • the announcements, requests, and comments you write;
  • read markers and subscriptions to discussions;
  • your device's push notification token, so we can send you notifications.

If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your name and profile photo from them, and your email address if you allowed them to share it. We never post anything on your behalf and have no access to your friends, follows, or messages.

Who can see your data

Your phone number is hidden from other residents by default. It becomes visible to them only if you turn that on yourself in profile settings ("Open").

  • Other residents see your name, profile photo, block, and apartment number next to your announcements, requests, and comments.
  • The management company additionally sees your phone number and car plates — to reach you in an emergency (flooding, fire, a car blocking access) and to handle your requests.
  • Nobody outside the complex sees your data. The app is available to residents only. Your data is not published openly, not sold, and not shared for advertising.

Who we share data with

We do not sell your data. To run the app we rely on the following providers, each receiving only the minimum it needs:

Provider What it receives Why
Google Firebase (Google Cloud) All profile data and app content Database and file storage, sign-in, and push notifications. Servers in Europe
smsc.kz Your phone number and the text of the code SMS only Sending the confirmation code when you sign in by phone
Anthropic (Claude) The text of announcements, requests, and comments Automatic translation of content into Russian, Kazakh, and English
Google, Meta (Facebook) The fact that you signed in Only if you chose to sign in with Google or Facebook

How long we keep data

Profile data is kept for as long as your account exists. SMS confirmation codes are deleted as soon as you sign in or when they expire (5 minutes). Announcements, requests, and comments are kept until you delete them or delete your account.

How to delete your data

You may request full deletion of your account and all data associated with it at any time. To do so:

  1. write or call the management company using the contact details below, mentioning "data deletion";
  2. give your phone number, block, and apartment number so we can find your record;
  3. we will delete your profile, photo, car plates, and notification tokens.

We process the request within 30 days and tell you once the deletion is done. Your announcements and comments are deleted together with your account; if you want a discussion to remain but your name removed, say so in the request.

If you signed in with Facebook or Google, removing the app in those services' settings stops them sending us data, but does not delete the profile already created here — that needs the request above.

Your rights

You have the right to know what data we hold about you, to correct it, to restrict its use, or to delete it. You can change most of it yourself on the "Settings" screen. For anything else, use the contact details below.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We will announce significant changes in the app.
